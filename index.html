FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FreeCalc really free?
Yes, completely. No subscription, no account, no hidden fees. FreeCalc is free forever and supported by Google advertising.
Does my data get stored anywhere?
No. Every calculation runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
How accurate are the currency rates?
The currency converter uses approximate rates for quick reference. Always verify with your bank before large transfers.
Can I use BMI for medical decisions?
BMI is a general screening tool, not a medical diagnosis. Consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
Are tax calculations accurate for my country?
The calculators are estimates for guidance only. Tax laws vary by country — always consult a professional accountant.
